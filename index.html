About

I write stories about people and the choices they make under difficult circumstances, and the consequences that follow. My work is often eclectic and self-reflective. The writing tends to be quiet and unresolved. I’m more interested in experience than explanation. This site is a small library of work written over time. There’s no schedule, no commentary, and no attempt to chase trends. The stories are here to be read slowly, or not at all. I publish under a pen name to keep the focus on the work itself. It’s not a disguise, just a boundary. If something here stays with you, that’s enough. — Alina M. Hart
